    What's stored on this device

    Your tasks, notes, coaching history, momentum log and profile are saved in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server except the messages you send to the Coach.

    What's sent to AI

    When you use the Coach, your board contents and conversation are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's standard data handling applies. Your data is not used to train models by default.

    Coach memory

    After each coaching session, Nudgley extracts a few insights about how you work. These stay on your device and are included in future coaching prompts to make them more relevant. You can clear this at any time.

    Nudgley is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, or replacement for professional support. AI-generated insights may be incomplete or inaccurate.
