Your ADHD task board
The hardest part isn't the work.
It's starting it.

If you have ADHD, your brain doesn't lack ideas — it lacks structure at the moment you need it most. Nudgley gives you that structure: instantly, without friction, without setup.

Speak your tasks out loud. They land on your board, sorted and ready. When you're stuck or overwhelmed, your Coach reads exactly what's on your board and tells you what to do next.

This isn't a wellness app. It's a tool for high-functioning professionals with ADHD who need a system that works as fast as they think.

Nudgley is not a medical device or a replacement for professional ADHD support.
